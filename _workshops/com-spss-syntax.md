## ’14 COM SPSS Syntax
Workshop on SPSS Programming (i.e., Syntax)

The workshop aims to train students to learn how to use SPSS syntax, instead of the pull-down menu, to transform variables, maniplate data files, and perform statistical analysis.
The knowledge and skills of SPSS syntax also serve a bridge to more flexible and powerful programming languages such as R.
