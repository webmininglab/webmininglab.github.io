# Zhu lectured at UAE University

Invited by [United Arab Emirates University](<https://www.uaeu.ac.ae/en/>) (UAEU) in [Al Ain](<https://www.google.com.hk/maps/place/Al+Ain+-+Abu+Dhabi+-+United+Arab+Emirates/@24.1933625,55.46666,10z/data=!3m1!4b1!4m5!3m4!1s0x3e8ab145cbd5a049:0xf56f8cea5bf29f7f!8m2!3d24.1301619!4d55.8023118?hl=en>), Jonathan Zhu visited and delivered two lectures there, one on “Big data, artificial intelligence and computational media: realities and hypers” to the undergraduate and graduate students in College of Humanities and Social Sciences and another on “Will computational social science bring about a paradigm shift” to the faculty members of the college. He was also interviewed by UAEU News, a student-run TV station, on issues ranging from robot news to personal privacy (news clip on [youtube](<https://youtu.be/uhDf2ncMrlw>)). ![1](https://webmininglab.github.io/assets/uploads/2017/12/1.jpg) ![1](https://webmininglab.github.io/assets/uploads/2017/12/2.jpg)
