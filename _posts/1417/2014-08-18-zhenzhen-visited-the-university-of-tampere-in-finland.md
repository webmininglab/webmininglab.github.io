# Zhenzhen visited the University of Tampere in Finland

Zhenzhen was invited to visit the University of Tampere between Aug 4 and 15. She gave a talk about network evolution in the Sino-Finnish Summer School on Social Media Data Analysis. 

![fig1](https://webmininglab.github.io/assets/uploads/2014/08/fig1.jpg)

![fig2_副本](https://webmininglab.github.io/assets/uploads/2014/08/fig2_副本.jpg)

The summer school was co-organized by the School of Communication, Media and Theatre and School of Information Sciences at the University of Tampere. Fifteen Finnish and Chinese scholars from the two fields had intensive and inspiring discussions.
