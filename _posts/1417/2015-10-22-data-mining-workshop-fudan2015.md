# Workshop on Data Mining for New Media in December at Fudan

The newly-formed [strategic partners](<http://weblab.com.cityu.edu.hk/blog/blog/2015/07/24/collaboration-with-fudan/>) between CityU Web Mining Lab and Fudan New Media Lab will organize a workshop on data mining for new media on Dec. 11-15, 2015 at Fudan University in Shanghai. The workshop will cover how to collect, process, analyze, and visualize new media data, by a joint-team from both labs. For more details, please visit: <http://www.xwxy.fudan.edu.cn/node2/fdxwxy/xyxw/u1ai106665.html>
