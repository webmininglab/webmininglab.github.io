# Three More PhDs from the Lab

Three members of the Web Mining Lab, Liang Hai, Qin Jie and Wang Chengjun, have obtained their doctorates in communication on November 11. Dr. Liang, Dr. Qin and Dr. Wang have contributed a lot to the Lab in the past several years. They proved their academic ability by fruitful academic achievements. Dr. Liang, Dr. Qin and Dr. Wang are now pursuing a scholarly life in universities in Hong Kong and Nanjing. ![Three more PhDs from the Lab](https://webmininglab.github.io/assets/uploads/2014/11/IMG_0005-e1415716201532.jpg)
