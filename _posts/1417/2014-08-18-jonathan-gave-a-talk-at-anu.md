# Jonathan gave a talk at Australian National University

On August 1, 2014, Jonathan Zhu gave a keynote speech at the Workshop on Studying the Chinese Internet, organized by the Australian Centre on China in the World and the Australian Demographic & Social Research Institute at Australian National University in Canberra. In the talk, Jonathan provided a review of the landscape of research on Chinese social media, to highlight what we know and what we don’t know from the existing body of literature. The workshop drew participants from China studies, computer science, and the social sciences. ![anu-speech](https://webmininglab.github.io/assets/uploads/2014/08/anu-speech.png) Jonathan (left) and Robert Ackland (organizer of the workshop)
