# Zhenzhen wins a Top Paper Award from the GCSC Division of ICA

Zhenzhen’s paper wins a Top Paper Award from the Global Communication and Social Change Division of ICA. The paper is titled as "An Empirical Test of the Academic Dependency Theory: Using the Field of Communication as an Example". It is one of the three highest scoring papers out of the 115 that the division received this year.
