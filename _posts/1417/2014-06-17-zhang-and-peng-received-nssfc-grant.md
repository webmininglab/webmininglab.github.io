# Zhang Lun and Winson Received CNY200K Grant from NSSFC

Zhang Lun and Winson have received a research grant for young scholars from the national social science foundation of China (NSSFC). According to the national planning office of philosophy and social science (NPOPSS), the average success rate for NSSFC2014 is 13.6%. Their 2-year research project, entitled as “ _Dynamics of Framing on Public Events in Social Media_(14CXW015)”, aims at exploring the evolving patterns of frames on given public events in social media. (ZL)
