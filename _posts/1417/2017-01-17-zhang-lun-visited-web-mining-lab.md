# Zhang Lun visited Web Mining Lab

Dr. Zhang Lun, who graduated from WML in 2012 and is currently an Associate Professor at Bejing Normal University, returned to WML for old times' sake on Jan 16, 2017. Current members of WML are also thrilled to listen to her reflections on working and doing research in mainland China. Zhang Lun gave some suggestions about learning and welcome everyone to embrace the trend of computational communication research. She discussed with Jonathan about future collaboration. Zhang Lun also held a Social Network Analysis Workshop at CUHK this month. ![WechatIMG2](https://webmininglab.github.io/assets/uploads/2017/01/WechatIMG2.jpeg)
