# Yafei and Lu Win Research Tuition Scholarship and Outstanding Academic Performance Award

Yafei and Lu have received Research Tuition Scholarships and Outstanding Academic Performance Awards from Chow Yei Ching Graduate School of City University of Hong Kong. The awards and scholarships are given to research students with both excellent course GPAs and outstanding academic publications, ranking 20% of the total research students. Awardees will be given a cash prize and an exemption of tuition fees.
