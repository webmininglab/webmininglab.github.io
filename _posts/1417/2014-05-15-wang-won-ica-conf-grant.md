# Chengjun and Zhenzhen received Student Travel Grant from the ICA Conference

Each year, ICA received a huge number of applications for travel funding. This year, Chengjun and Zhenzhen were awarded the Student Travel Grant from the ICA Conference 2014. The conference will be held in Seattle. On the conference, Chengjun will present two papers and Zhenzhen will present one paper. The titles of the papers are listed below: Wang, C. J. (2014). Information diffusion on microblogs: Testing the threshold hypothesis of interpersonal effects. Wang, C. J. (2014). The origin of bursts in public attention: The temporality hypothesis for the diffusion of YouTube videos. Wang, Z. Z. (2014). Mapping the landscape of communication research around the world: Globalization, civilization, hegemony, or interdependence?
