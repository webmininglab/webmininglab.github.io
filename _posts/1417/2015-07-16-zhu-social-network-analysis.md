# Jonathan’s chapter on social network analysis published

The chapter, coauthored by Robert Ackland of Australian National University and Jonathan Zhu, has appeared in [Innovations in Digital Research Methods](<https://uk.sagepub.com/en-gb/eur/innovations-in-digital-research-methods/book236862#contents>), edited by Peter Halfpenny and Rob Procter. The edited volume from Sage covers a wide range of computational methods, tools, and algorithms for social sciences, with the chapter by Ackland and Zhu specifically focused on social network analysis. ![digital_research](https://webmininglab.github.io/assets/uploads/2015/07/digital_research.png)
