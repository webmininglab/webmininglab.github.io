# Winson appointed as a Corresponding Member of the CCF Task Force on Big Data

Winson has been recently appointed by China Computer Federation (CCF) as a Corresponding Members of the CCF Task Force on Big Data (TFBD). Winson is among the 47 newly recruited Corresponding Members who were appointed based on a majority of votes of the existing members of CCF TFBD. Winson is also the first Singapore-based representative appointed within the CCF TFBD. Other members of the Task Force include experts from Australia, Canada, China, Hong Kong, United Kingdom and United States. Dr. Jonathan Zhu, the founding director of the Web Mining Lab, has served as an overseas member on the CCF TFBD since 2012.
