# The Lab Runs a Project in CityU Shenzhen Institute

![sri](https://webmininglab.github.io/assets/uploads/2016/10/sri.jpg) Jonathan Zhu and Hai Liang have launched a new project on social media and national images, in collaboration with Prof. Yan Ge at Shanghai Jiaotong University, with funding from the National Social Science Fund of China (NSSFC). The project is housed in CityU Shenzhen Research Institute (<http://sri.cityu.edu.hk/sri/>), which provides ample space and quick access to staffing and other research resources.
