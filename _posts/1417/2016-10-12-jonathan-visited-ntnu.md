# Jonathan Visited NTNU

![ntnu1](https://webmininglab.github.io/assets/uploads/2016/10/NTNU1.jpg) On Oct 5, Jonathan Zhu gave a talk on computational communication research in big data era ([slides](<http://weblab.com.cityu.edu.hk/workshops/other_talks/CCR_in_Big_Data_Era.pdf>)) at the Research Institute of Mass Communication, National Taiwan Normal University (NTNU) in Taipei. The NTNU Institute has been a pioneering program in Taiwan in offering graduate-level training on computational methods. Dr. Tosti Chiang (the first from the right) is currently teaching a course on news games in which students design online games for news reporting.
