# Dr. Sihui Tang joins the lab

Dr. Tang, an associate professor of industrial engineering at South China University of Technology (SCUT), has arrived since March 17, as a visiting scholar for 6 months. Dr. Tang teaches and conducts research on management information systems, e-Commerce, and customer relationship management. She will be collaborating with our interdisciplinary team to study interdisciplinary collaboration. Her visit is funded by SCUT, a leading university in Guangzhou. ![dr.tang](https://webmininglab.github.io/assets/uploads/2014/03/dr.tang_.jpg) (JZ)
