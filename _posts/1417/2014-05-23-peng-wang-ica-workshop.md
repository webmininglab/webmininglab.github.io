# Winson and Zhenzhen delivered a workshop on text mining in ICA preconference

Winson and Zhenzhen represented the lab and gave an one-hour methodology workshop in the ICA preconference titled as "New Media and Citizenship in Asia". The workshop is about text mining for new media research. It consists four parts, including collection, pre-processing, analysis, and visualization of text data.
