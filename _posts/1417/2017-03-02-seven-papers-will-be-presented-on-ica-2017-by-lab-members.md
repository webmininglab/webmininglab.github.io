# Lab members will give seven presentations on ICA 2017

The 67th Annual ICA conference will be held on May 25-29, 2017 in San Diego, USA. On the conference, lab members will give seven presentations in six different sessions. The titles of the papers are listed below: Zhu, J. J. H., Guan, L., Liang, H. & Peng, T. Q. (2017). [New Bottle of Old Wine vs. Old Bottle of New Wine: What Contributions Have Computational Methods Made to Social Media Research?](<http://tinyurl.com/zwrbtgz>) Peng, T. Q. & Zhu, J. J. H. (2017). [It’s not just about mobility: How can mobility datasets advance our understanding of information diffusion?](<http://tinyurl.com/j45g3lj>) Zhang, L., Peng, T. Q. & Zheng, L. (2017). [Technologically Narrowing but Structurally Diversifying: News Consumption on Mobile Internet](<http://tinyurl.com/hjkxtld>). Zhang, Y. F., Guan, L., Chen, H. X., & Zhu, J. J. H. (2017). [Using Text Mining to Measure Diffusion of Innovation](<http://tinyurl.com/z8lg7uj>). Liang, H. (2017). [The Implications of Computational Methods for Comparative Studies](<http://tinyurl.com/jumv4uf>). Zhang, L. (2017). [Divergence or Convergence: Interaction between News Media Frames and Public Frames in Online Discussion Forum in China](<http://tinyurl.com/jxbuqo9>). Wang, C. J. & Zhang, X. Z. (2017). [Analyzing Mobile Phone Data with Network Science](<http://tinyurl.com/jlac4lw>).
