# Liang and Zhu's chapter on social media data collection published

A chapter “Big Data, Collection of (Social Media, Harvesting)”, coauthored by Hai Liang and Jonathan Zhu of the Lab, has been published in  _[The International Encyclopedia of Communication Research Methods](<http://as.wiley.com/WileyCDA/WileyTitle/productCd-1118901762.html>)_ , edited by Jörg Matthes and published by John Wiley & Sons. The chapter summarizes sampling and data collection methods on social media (see [Liang_Zhu_2017_Big data](<https://webmininglab.github.io/assets/uploads/2017/12/Liang_Zhu_2017_Big-data.pdf>)).
