# PhD Student Qin Jie Published a Translated Version of Media Criticism Classics

Qin Jie, a second-year PhD student funded by Hong Kong PhD Fellowship, has published her first Chinese translation, _Games and Activities for Media, Communication, and Cultural Studies Students_ , originally authored by Arthur A. Berger in 2004. The translated version is published by Tsinghua University Press in Beijing. The book is among the series of Media Criticism Classics, edited by Dr. Wang Junchao, an Associate Professor at School of Journalism and Communication in Tsinghua University, who highly regards the value of the original book and the quality of the translation. Another book by Berger, _Making Sense of Media: Key Texts in Media and Cultural Studies_ , has also been translated by Qin Jie and will be published as an entry of the Media Criticism Classics soon.
