# One More PhD from the Lab

After three years of hard working, Zhang Lun finally received her PhD Degree in Communication on November 7. She presented part of her dissertation research at a departmental research seminar ([click here to see the slides](<http://newmedia4.cityu.edu.hk/videostream/seminar/2011/20110919.pdf>)). She is the sixth doctoral graduate trained by Jonathan Zhu at City University of Hong Kong.
