# Peng &amp; Zhu&rsquo;s paper on internet and sociability published

[![newmedia&society-cover](https://webmininglab.github.io/assets/uploads/2011/06/newmediasociety-cover1.gif)](<http://nms.sagepub.com/content/13/4.toc>)Peng, T. Q., & Zhu, J. J. H. (2011). A game of win-win or win-lose? Revisiting the internet’s influence on sociability and use of traditional media. _New Media & Society_, 13 (4), 568-586. This study examines the influence of internet adoption and internet usage on sociability and use of traditional media. Find more details in [abstract](<http://nms.sagepub.com/content/13/4/568>).
