# New Look of the Lab

The Web Mining Lab got a new look today. Since it moved into the landmark Run Run Shaw Creative Media Center (CMC), the Lab has established a series of improvement plans, including logo design, equipment upgrade, furniture renewal, and so on. [![](https://webmininglab.github.io/assets/uploads/2011/11/IMG_7328.jpg)](<https://webmininglab.github.io/assets/uploads/2011/11/IMG_7328.jpg>) _Above: Each desk carries a quarter of the logo. Four desks combine into a complete design. This creative idea came from Professor Jonathan Zhu, founder of the Lab (Third from left)._
