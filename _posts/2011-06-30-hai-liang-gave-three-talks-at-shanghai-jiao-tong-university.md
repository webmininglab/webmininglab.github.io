# Hai Liang Gave Three Talks at Shanghai Jiao Tong University

Hai Liang, a second year PhD student in our lab, was invited to give three talks at Shanghai Jiao Tong University in last [![](https://webmininglab.github.io/assets/uploads/2011/06/gf.jpg)](<http://weblab.com.cityu.edu.hk/blog/blog/2011/06/30/hai-liang-gave-three-talks-at-shanghai-jiao-tong-university/gf/>)week. Hai gave a talk on _framing research in communication_ for the research students at Institute of Arts and Humanities, and organized a seminar talk on his recent study on _behavior transition and repetition_ at Antai College of Economics & Management on 22nd June. On 27th June, Hai talked with a research group in School of Information Security Engineering on _survey study for massive data storage_. In addition to the talks, they also discussed the possibility for future collaboration in terms of data collection and sharing.
