# Graduate Students  of PKUSZ Visited Web Mining Lab

[![](https://webmininglab.github.io/assets/uploads/2011/06/CUHK012.jpg)](<http://weblab.com.cityu.edu.hk/blog/?attachment_id=529>)In the afternoon of June 22, the students majoring in communication from ShenZhen graduate school of Peking university (PKUSZ) visited media and communication department and web mining lab. They had a discussion with Jonathan Zhu. The Peking University Shenzhen Graduate School ( PKUSZ) was established as a satellite graduate campus of Peking University in 2001 via a joint venture with the Shenzhen Municipal Government. It is situated inside the University Town of Shenzhen, along with the graduate schools of Tsinghua University and the Harbin Institute of Technology. Jonathan Zhu introduced the status of the communication education in Hong Kong and the programs of city university of Hong Kong, including the issues of employment, master programs, and doctor programs. [![](https://webmininglab.github.io/assets/uploads/2011/06/CUHK07.jpg)](<http://weblab.com.cityu.edu.hk/blog/?attachment_id=530>) [](<http://weblab.com.cityu.edu.hk/blog/?attachment_id=530>)After the tour, the visiting students exchanged souvenirs with Jonathan Zhu, and had group photos taken together.
