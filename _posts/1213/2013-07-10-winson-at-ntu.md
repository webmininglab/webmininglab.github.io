# Winson Peng Starts New Life at NTU, Singapore

On July 1, 2013, Tai-Quan Peng (“Winson”) joined as an assistant professor in the Wee Kim Wee School of Communication and Information, Nanyang Technological University at Singapore. Prior to that, he was an assistant professor at Macau University of Science and Technology for four years. Winson got his PhD degree in 2008 from City University of Hong Kong under the supervision of Prof. Jonathan Zhu and worked as a research fellow at Web Mining Lab for one year. Over the past five years, Winson has published several articles with members of the Lab in SSCI/SCI-listed journals, including _New Media and Society_ , _Journal of the American Society for Information Science and Technology_ , _Scientometrics_ , _Computers in Human Behavior_ , _Information, Communication and Society_ and _Cyberpsychology & Behavior_. ![Winson Photo at NTU](https://webmininglab.github.io/assets/uploads/2013/07/Winson-Photo-at-NTU-1.jpg) (TQP)
