# Chengjun won First-place Prize at ASDSC2013

Chengjun Wang received the First-place Prize of Student Paper Competition (English-language Track) from Asian Symposium of Doctoral Students in Communication ([ASDSC2013](<http://com.cityu.edu.hk/ASDSC2013/>)), held at City University of Hong Kong Nov 18, 2013, for his paper on [Information Diffusion on Microblogs: Testing Threshold Hypothesis of Interpersonal Effects](<http://newmedia4.cityu.edu.hk/ASDSC-Nov2013/CityU/asdsc2013_abstract_9_Wang%20Chengjun.pdf>) ([**slides**](<http://chengjun.github.io/slides/strut/weibo/>)). Participants in ASDSC2013 include doctoral students from City University of Hong Kong, Communication University of China, Fudan University (China), Nanyang Technological University (Singapore), National Taiwan University, and Yonsei University (Korea).
