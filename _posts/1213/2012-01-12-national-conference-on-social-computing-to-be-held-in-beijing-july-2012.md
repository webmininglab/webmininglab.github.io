# National Conference on Social Computing to be held in Beijing, November 2012

The 4th China National Conference on Social Computing (NCSC2012), to be held in Beijing, November 16-17, 2012, is calling for paper submission, with the deadline on July 16 (<http://idke.ruc.edu.cn/ncsc2012>). The conference will focus on the role of social computing in computer science, management science, and social sciences. Xiaofeng Meng of [Lab of Web and Mobile Data Management](<http://idke.ruc.edu.cn/wamdm/>) at Renmin University of China and Jonathan Zhu of [Web Mining Lab](<http://weblab.com.cityu.edu.hk/>) at City University of Hong Kong will co-chair the conference.
