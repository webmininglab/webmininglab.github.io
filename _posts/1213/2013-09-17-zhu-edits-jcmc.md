# Jonathan joins editorial board of JCMC

Jonathan Zhu is invited to join the editorial board of _J_ _ournal of Computer-Mediated Communication_ , a Q1 journal in SSCI-Communication, for which he served a guest co-editor of a special issue on Internet in Asia. He also sits on the editorial board of three other SSCI-Communication journals, including _Communication Research_ , _International Journal of Public Opinion Research_ , and _Asian Journal of Communication_. Previously, he served on the editorial board of _Human Communication Research_ and _Political Communication,_ respectively _._

(JZ)
