# Zhu appointed to serve on CCF Task Force on Big Data

Jonathan Zhu has been appointed by China Computer Federation (CCF) an overseas member of the newly formed Task Force on Big Data ([TFBD](<http://www.ccf.org.cn/sites/ccf/xhdtnry.jsp?contentId=2695711538901>)). Among the 70 members, 44 are from universities in mainland China, 16 from industry, and 10 from overseas. The body will include four working groups in charge of conference organization, scholarly exchange, industry liaison, and open source community. ![](https://webmininglab.github.io/assets/uploads/2012/09/image002.png)
