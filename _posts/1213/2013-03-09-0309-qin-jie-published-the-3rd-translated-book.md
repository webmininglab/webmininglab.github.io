# Qin Jie published the 3rd translated book

Qin Jie has published her third Chinese translation of _Cultural Studies: The Basics_ , originally authored by Jeff Lewis in 2008. The translated version, coauthored with Zhenzhi Guo, is published by Tsinghua University Press. The book is part of the Series of Translated Textbooks in Journalism and Communication, edited by Guo, a Professor of School of Journalism and Communication at Tsinghua University. During her two-and-a-half year studies in the lab, Qin has published two other translated books, both authored by Arthur Berger: _Games and Activities for Media, Communication, and Cultural Studies Students_ , and _Making Sense of Media: Key Texts in Media and Cultural Studies_. ![Qin Jie 3rd Book](https://webmininglab.github.io/assets/uploads/2013/03/IMG_20130308_101239-225x300.jpg)
