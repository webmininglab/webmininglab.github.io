# Jonathan coauthors a review on social computing in the era of big data

A review on opportunities and challenges for social computing in the era of big data has been published by Xiaofeng Meng, Yong Li, and Jonathan Zhu in [_Journal of Computer Research and Development_](<http://crad.ict.ac.cn/recommendpaper/131201.pdf>). The review, in Chinese language, was a by-product from the 2012 International Conference on Social Computing cochaired by Meng and Zhu in October 2012 at Renmin University of China.
