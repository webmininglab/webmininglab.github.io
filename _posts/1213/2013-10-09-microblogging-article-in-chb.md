# New article on microblogging to appear in Computers in Human Behavior

Zhang Lun et al. have a new article, “Content or context: Which matters more in information processing on microblogging sites?”, accepted for publication by _Computers in Human Behavior_. The study is based on a collaboration project with Sina Research Institute, where Zhang Lun interned as a doctoral student.
