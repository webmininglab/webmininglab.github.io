# Zhijin Promoted to Associate Professor

![](https://webmininglab.github.io/assets/uploads/2012/06/zhijin-zhong.jpg)Dr. Zhijin Zhong (<http://scd.sysu.edu.cn/Item/189.aspx>), a graduate of the Lab in 2009, was recently promoted to Associate Professor in School of Communication and Design, San Yat-sen University in Guangzhou, China. Zhijin has authored/coauthored the following four articles in SSCI-journals, along with other publications: Zhong, Z. J. (2009). [Third-Person Perceptions and Online Games: A Comparison of Perceived Antisocial and Pro-social Game Effects](<http://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1111/j.1083-6101.2009.01441.x/abstract>). Journal of Computer-Mediated Communication, 14(2), 286-306. Zhong, Z. J. (2011). [From Access to Usage: the Divide of Self-reported Digital Skills among Adolescents](<http://www.mendeley.com/research/access-usage-divide-selfreported-digital-skills-among-adolescents-1/>). Computers & Education, 56(3), 736-746. Zhong, Z. J. (2011). [The Effects of Collective MMORPG (Massively Multiplayer Online Role-Playing Games) Play on Gamers' Online and Offline Social Capital](<http://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S074756321100152X>). Computers in Human Behavior, 27, 2352-2363. Peng, T. Q., Zhang, L., Zhong, Z. J., & Zhu, J. J. H. (in press). [Mapping the landscape of Internet studies: Text mining of social science journal articles 2000-2009](<http://weblab.com.cityu.edu.hk/blog/blog/2012/06/06/paper-on-internet-studies-2000-09-accepted-by-nms/>). New Media & Society.
