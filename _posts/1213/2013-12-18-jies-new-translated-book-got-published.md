# Jie’s new translated book got published

Qin Jie's latest translated book, _[Making Sense of Media: Key Texts in Media and Cultural Studies](<http://as.wiley.com/WileyCDA/WileyTitle/productCd-1405120169.html>)_ , authored by Arthur Asa Berger, Professor Emeritus of Broadcast and Electronic Communication Arts at San Francisco State University, got published by Tsinghua University Press. During her three-year studies in the lab, Jie has translated and published [three books](<http://www6.cityu.edu.hk/com/Page_Frame.aspx?type=news&id=160>) on the teaching and research in media and cultural studies.
