# Winson Peng Secures Research Grant from MSRA

Winson Peng is awarded the Urban Informatics Research Grant from Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA). He will collaborate with researchers at MSRA to carry out a study on “Tracking Dynamics of Social Happiness on Twitter”. (Winson)
