# Jonathan’s talk on education in big data age published

Jonathan Zhu published an article on “Education for journalism and communication in the age of big data: Curriculum design, student skills, and faculty recruitment” in the latest issue of _Journalism University_ , a scholarly journal in Chinese language edited by Fudan University Journalism School (<http://www.xwxy.fudan.edu.cn/node2/fdxwxy/xsyj/node823/node965/userobject1ai103095.html>). The article was based on his presentation at the symposium on challenges and opportunities for education in journalism and communication at Fudan in December 2012. (JZ)
