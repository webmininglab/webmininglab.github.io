# CONGRATULATIONS TO THREE LAB MEMBERS ON THEIR GRADUATION AND JOB OFFER

Three members of our lab, Zhang Yafei, Lan Ji, and Chen Zhicong, have recently graduated from their PhD studies at City University of Hong Kong (CityU).

Yafei successfully obtained his joint-PhD degrees from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in December 2021 and from CityU in June 2022.

![](https://weblab.com.cityu.edu.hk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/image-2.png)

Lan Ji received his PhD degree in Computer Science from Zhejiang University in June 2022 and will obtain his joint Ph.D. degree in Media and Communication from CityU in November 2022.

![](https://weblab.com.cityu.edu.hk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/image-1.png)

Zhicong will also obtain his PhD from CityU in November this year.

![](https://weblab.com.cityu.edu.hk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/image.png)

Currently, Yafei and Lan Ji are working as postdoctoral researchers at Laboratory for AI-Powered Financial Technologies and Zhicong will start his postdoc position at Nanjing University.
