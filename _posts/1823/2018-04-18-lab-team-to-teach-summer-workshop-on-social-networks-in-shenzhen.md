# Lab Team to Teach Summer Workshop on Social Networks in Shenzhen

Jonathan Zhu, Winson Peng, and Hai Liang will teach a 5-day workshop at Shenzhen University (SZU) from July 4 to July 9 (<http://cmc.szu.edu.cn/Article/ShowArticle.asp?ArticleID=2799>). Co-organized by SZU School of Communication and CityU Centre for Communication Research (CCR), the 2018 Computational Communication Research Workshop will focus on how to apply social network analysis to communication research. The workshop will be taught in Chinese, free of charge whereas participants should cover their own travel/lodging expenses. Those who are interested should follow the instructions on the above website and submit the application by April 30. Participants will be selected on a competitive basis.
