# Xiaofan receives a  CityU internal grant on cryptocurrency market monitoring project

Dr. LIU Xiaofan receives a grant for building a cryptocurrency market monitoring platform from the CityU’s knowledge transfer initiative. 

The project aims at continuously surveilling (both licit and illicit) activities in the cryptocurrency economy such as investor behavior, market manipulation, money laundering, and other financial risks, by collecting, integrating, and analyzing blockchain transactions and Internet data. 

This project marks a further step taken by the Web Mining Lab towards an interdisciplinary research agenda in understanding human behavior from online traces.
