# Winson and Hai Joined JOC Leadership

_Journal of Communication (JOC)_ , the flagship publication of International Communication Association (ICA), has recently formed a new leadership. Two of our Lab are part of the new[ team](<https://academic.oup.com/joc/pages/Editorial_Board>).

Winson Peng serves an Associate Editor and Hai Liang is a member of the Editorial Board. Congratulations!

Jonathan Zhu has retired from the Editorial Board after his five-year services.
