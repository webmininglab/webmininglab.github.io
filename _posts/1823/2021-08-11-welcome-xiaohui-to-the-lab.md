# Welcome Xiaohui to the Lab

The lab has a new faculty member -- [Dr. Xiaohui (Vincent) Wang](<http://www.cityu.edu.hk/com/Profile.aspx?u=xwan26>) who has recently joined the Department of Media and Communication at CityU as an assistant professor. Vincent received his PhD in information studies from Nanyang Technological University and was a research assistant professor at Hong Kong Baptist University before coming to CityU. 

![](https://weblab.com.cityu.edu.hk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/Vincent_Wang_20210802.jpeg)

Vincent's research converges at the intersection of information, communication, and new technologies, with a focus on social media, computational social science, consumer health informatics, and human-computer interaction.

Welcome to the lab, Vincent.
