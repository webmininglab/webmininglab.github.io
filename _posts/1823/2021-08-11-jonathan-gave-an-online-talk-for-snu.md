# Jonathan gave an online talk for SNU

Jonathan Zhu of the lab gave a talk, through Zoom, on Aug 3 to faculty members and students at Seoul National University (SNU), Korea. The talk was part of the [Overseas Scholar Colloquium in Journalism](<https://icr.snu.ac.kr/en/board/international/view/6156>) organized by the Institute of Communication Research at SNU.

![](https://weblab.com.cityu.edu.hk/blog/wp-content/uploads/2021/08/Mapping-Social-Media-Research-300x169.png)

In the 90-minute talk on Computational Methods and Social Media, Jonathan presented preliminary findings from his ongoing review of the evolution and the state of art of computational research in the field.
