# Fan Xiaojiang heads Mingpao News in New York

Fan Xiaojiang, an MPhil graduate in 2004, has been promoted to Director of News Department, Mingpao Daily in New York. Miss Fan came to CityU in 2001 with a BA in Journalism from Renmin University of China. While studying at CityU, she carried out experimental research on effects of online newspapers under the supervision of Prof Jonathan Zhu. She has since graduation been working for Mingpao New York, one of the leading Chinese language newspapers in North America.
